About

Here at Handy Zeb, we're building a technology-enabled platform connecting customers with independent service provider professionals to foster a better home service experience. We were founded in 2017 in Baguio by Zebedee Jimenez and we're headquartered in Green Valley. Currently, Handy Zeb offers a range of services in most major cities across the Philippines.



Our mission is to consistently bring you the best services with the best quality, by keeping up with the current trends and constantly educating ourselves about the best practices , keeping your home safe with the meh prices while still being able to do quality work for you to subscribe to us again.
