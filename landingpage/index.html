About

Handyzeb has been providing home owners, contractors and service providers with quality matching services and helpful resources since April 2017. We ourselves are homeowners, contractors and users of handyzeb.org to fill our own need to find quality contractors to do property management and home improvement with our commercial and residential real-estate holdings. We know how frustrating it can be in finding quality and respectable handyman contractors and will continue to bring new products and services to our site to make your experience an enjoyable one.



Have you heard of instagram or facebook? Well forget social networking, we are focused on you, the consumer. We are becoming the industry standard "consumer networking" portal. We have recently introduced our customized web page offering, free for a limited time, to home owners, realtors and contractors. HandyZeb is becoming a catalyst product for our customers to help streamline communications, rate/review service providers, update and track work progress and much more. Handyzeb is your home improvement and property management best friend if you want to save time, frustration and get the job done. We have many exciting future developments planned. We are here to serve you and strive to bring products and service to market that make your life easier when trying to improve or maintain your most important asset, your property. Contact us webmaster at handyzeb dot org anytime with your comments or questions and please visit us on Saint Louis University, Bonifacio St. Baguio City. See you!
